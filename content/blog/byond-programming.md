 So I began learning how to program DM, Dream Maker (The Language Byond uses)
and its been very fun. I have seen this a very long time ago and wasn't very
appealing at the time haha.

Byond stands for Build Your Own Net Dream and that's what I'm going to do. I
began going through some documentation and watching a few videos to get a hang
of things and I think I'll be comfortable enough with it by the end of the
year to publish something. I will most likely use this for Game Jam 2023.
