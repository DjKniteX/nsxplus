Alright guys, I have decided to bring back The Gaiuss trilogy but with some major changes! If you guys have been following me for the
past 10 or so years, I was working on a set of games in RPG Maker MV but lost the files for the 2nd game, the 3rd game was a failed attempt at an online RPG, but my first game which is still available, Cartuga is playable. Fun fact, that game also help me land my first Dev job when I presented it as my final for my Front-End coding course. 

My main goal for this trilogy was that each game will have different mechanics and techniques used to show that progression I was making as an aspiring developer. The same concept will all apply with some new things that I have learned over the years. Cartuga is being remade still in MV and that is almost done! The Original Files will still be online on my Github. Gralina and Embellos will be done in MZ. 

The games will be released to be played via web and mobile on itch.io but the desktop versions such as Windows/Linux will have their own custom launcher!

I have decided to pivot Bastille to be a short single player RPG that may or may not be related. But I am going to plan out the roguelite rpg to be a sequel to Bastille. But for now, enjoy these quick screenshots of the the newly updated Cartuga
