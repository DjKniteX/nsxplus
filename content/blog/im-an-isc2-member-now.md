 I took and passed my test on the same day (July 15) and received my
certificate the next day. 50$ a year isn't that bad especially since the
program makes you get CPE (continue education) to keep your certificates. I
like this approach because I feel like many people earn certs and when they
don't get a job they just let it sit there and they are not doing anything to
level up or keep up with the trend.

This makes me wanna attend webinars and read articles to get my 15 CPEs a
year. I know its more when you get your CISSP but hopefully at that time I'll
be ready to contribute to the world out there.

Anyways this is nice because now it's one less class for me and I'm feeling
more accomplished than I ever been when I started on this security journey a
few years ago. It's time to keep going.

I'll be working on my own personal projects once I get a new computer. (A
dedicated computer to work on stuff).

See you soon.
