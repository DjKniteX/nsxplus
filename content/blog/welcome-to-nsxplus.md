love of the MSX computers. I am rebranding to do a lot of retro coding which I

Named after my favorite coding community  
  
 Mirage Wars is dope wars clone that is part of a set of text-based games for
the C64 which I hope to end the series with a full-fledged game made in
Assembly.

You play as Simon, a Source Runner looking to sell stolen source codes
throughout the community to make sure organizations such as the X'lithan
Organization take it for their own personal gain. You buy and sell bits of a
source code so no one has the full source making it harder for the corrupted
corporations to take and use.

Anyways, that's it for now. Looking to start a youtube channel soon and will
